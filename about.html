Schnabeltiere - Fat Plat

Schnabeltiere is a collection of NFTs created by nebukadnezar. Inspired by the market and general crypto sentiment, the Fat Plats were born. 4096 unique digital collectible were created, generated with python and deployed to Polygon.

Schnabeltiere is the german word for platypuses and was chosen for its charisma. Mint your own version now!

The smartcontract implements the Opensea.io ERC721Tradable standard. This means, after you mint one, you can sell it on the opensea marketplace, without paying the initial trading fee. All my used code is open source and linked below. Feel free to have a look, use it for yourself, provide me some feedback or suggest some changes.
